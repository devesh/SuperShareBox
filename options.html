Usage

Just share on Google+ to your circles as normal.

Optionally, also add a friend or circle from your other social networks like

When you do, Super Share Box will automatically post a link on that other social network to your Google+ post. Remember that the "circles" from other social networks just determine who will see the link to this post on that other social network. For them to see the actual post, they must be in one of the Google+ circles you've shared it to.

Settings

Share Settings
Enabled Networks
Post Truncation
If you want to increase interactions on Google+, you should truncate the posts you share to the other social networks to give the viewers an incentive to click over to your Google+ post and interact with you there. Uncheck the truncate box if you want to share the full post (discouraged).
Google Analytics
Enable Google Analytics
SuperShareBox uses Google Analytics to track the number of posts and failures and alert the developer if the failure rate gets too high.
